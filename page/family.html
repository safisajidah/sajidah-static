A Circle of Support

I come from a close-knit family that’s always supportive and full of laughter. We truly know how to enjoy life together! Every year, we hold a Family Day with our extended family, and it’s a highlight for all of us.

One of my favorite memories is our trip to Tioman Island. The stunning coral reefs and crystal-clear waters made for an unforgettable adventure! It was my first time snorkeling, and I’ll never forget the thrill of swimming in the middle of the sea, surrounded by vibrant marine life.

Through these family gatherings, I've learned the true meaning of family and kindness. My family has always been there to teach me the importance of supporting one another, sharing good times, and creating lasting memories. We may not always have it all together, but together, we have it all!
